About us

We are a group of students passionate about Artificial Intelligence and dedicated to exploring its potential. Our current project focuses on utilizing deep learning techniques to count people from an image file. Through our work, we aim to showcase the power of AI in real-world applications. As we continue to improve and evolve our project, we plan to implement better models, enable video capture and counting, and develop real-time analysis capabilities to enhance the overall performance and versatility of the system.

For more information or inquiries, feel free to contact us!
